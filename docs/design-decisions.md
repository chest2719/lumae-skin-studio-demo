# Lumae Design Direction

The approved homepage is the visual source of truth.

## Core direction

- Light, warm premium-beauty atmosphere
- Serif-led campaign typography with restrained sans-serif interface copy
- A strong split hero with treatment photography and a circular Skin Start badge
- Four concise trust principles directly beneath the hero
- Concern navigation presented as a compact beauty-service index
- Skin Start as the central commercial offer
- Three Signature Treatments in a clean editorial card row
- Burgundy as the primary conversion color; peach and ivory as the supporting palette

## Guardrails

- No fake press logos, awards or before/after results
- No product-shop framing
- No decorative 3D objects or excessive motion
- No medical diagnosis language
- No further art-direction changes without updating the approved mock-up first
